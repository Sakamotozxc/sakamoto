About Us

Welcome to Barangay Sugod, a pristine tourist destination nestled in the heart of Barangay Santan. Our community-driven tourism initiative offers visitors an authentic experience of Filipino hospitality and natural beauty.

We are committed to sustainable tourism practices that benefit both our visitors and local community. Our tourist spot features a beautiful boardwalk, cottage accommodations, and various recreational activities suitable for families and groups.

Experience the warmth of our local culture while enjoying the serenity of our natural surroundings. We welcome visitors with open arms and ensure a memorable stay for everyone.
